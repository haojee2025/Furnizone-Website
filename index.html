Furnizone Industries Sdn Bhd began its humble beginnings in 2009 as Malaysia’s knockdown particle board (engineered wood) furniture manufacturer. Today the business has bloomed, with almost 100 employees and a factory that’s 7,000 square meters.

We have an excellent team in research and development to customize trendy pieces of wardrobe, bedroom sets, children sets, living room furniture, children's cabinet, kitchen cabinet, and hallway furniture.

Our company has built a reputation as a reputable panel board base furniture maker for over the decade. Our success is due not only to the company’s efficient ability to produce the goods ordered with a short manufacturing lead time but also to the credit of the R&D process, which allows the market to experience creative and trendy goods. In addition, 90% of the items are shipped to other countries.

We manufacture our furniture on a vast scale with superior furniture manufacturing technologies. In our factory, we have a variety of high-tech machines, including a high-speed auto panel saw machine, a CNC machine, a high-end edge banding machine, and automatic boring machines. In addition, our business has a smooth conveyor system that delivers excellent production while reducing error and lost time.

Material & Color Options

FURNIZONE PARTICLE BOARD

Customize Your Furniture To Match Your Aesthetic

Our furniture is made using high-quality raw materials, such as Engineered Wood (Particle board and MDF board). We use particle board as the primary material in our company's furniture design, For instance, wardrobes, bedroom sets, children's cabinets, and DIY kitchen cabonets.

We also manufacture and export bespoke furniture to a number of nations, all of which are based on research to provide products that meet local preferences and cultural norms. The wood surfaces are ideal for all types of lamination, such as PVC, paper, and melamine. We pay close attention to all of our customers' requirements, and we endeavor to create a work of art for each piece.
